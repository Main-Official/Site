Overview

Diski Nine9 Africa is a registered non-profit company based in Soweto, South Africa (Reg no: 2015/447508/08). Diski Nine9 was formed in 2015 and our objective is to use sport, notably soccer and netball as a tool to educate and empower young Africans.“Diski” is a slang term used in South Africa to describe the biggest and most loved sport around the world – Soccer. “Nine9” is a slang term meaning Authentic or Real.

What we do

In response to social ills that have negative effects in underprivileged communities, Co-founders, Raymond Ledwaba and Phehello Monamodi established Diski Nine9 as a way to change the narrative by providing youths from Soweto with a platform to play sports in a safe place and realise their talents to become better professionals, leaders and role models in their communities. The following are the programmes they use to counteract these social ills:

  • After School Programme
  • School & Sports Holiday Programme
  • Sports Hospitality Academy
  • Greater Protea Netball League

Mission

Our Mission is to use the game of soccer and netball as tools to empower and educate young Africans.

Vision

Our Vision is to identify and nurture the next generation of responsible, healthy and empowered citizens who will contribute positively to building their communities.

Objectives

Create a safe environment that fosters friendships, encourages play and nurtures young talent.


Provide educational & scholarship opportunities for youths.


Provide employment opportunities to youths across sports value chain (i.e. marketing & sponsorship, hospitality, merchandising, transport, project & event management etc)
